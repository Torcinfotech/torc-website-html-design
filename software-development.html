The process of designing, developing, deploying, and supporting software for a specific group of users, functions, or organisations is known as custom software development.

In-house development teams or third-party developers often handle custom software development. Custom software development follows the same methods and methodologies as other types of software development. A custom project would follow the same processes as a standard project, such as gathering requirements, writing code, testing, and deploying, and would use the same methodologies, such as Agile and DevOp.
